Dundee's first zero-waste store

At The Little Green Larder, the aim is to encourage people to live a more sustainable and plastic free lifestyle while offering them ways to do so.

We offer Fresh food, dried foods, whole foods plastic free for you to fill into your own container, and we also do zero waste swaps for every day items. We are also known to host workshops teaching eco-friendly methods.

We have been noted by publications such as The Guardian, The Evening Telegraph and The Courier.
